About us

Our boat club

Our Boat Club is a unique, family-friendly marina located on the Hillsborough River in Tampa, Florida. The club provides members with access to a variety of amenities, including boat storage, boat ramp access, and dockside services. Our boat club also offers a variety of recreational activities, such as fishing trips, day cruises, and kayak trips. In addition, the club offers classes and workshops to teach members the basics of boating safety and maintenance. The Tampa Boat Club provides an opportunity for members to connect with others who share the same passion for boating and the outdoors.
